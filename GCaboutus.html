History of GHIRARDELLI'S

Ghirardelli's was formed by Michel Mark and his three sons, Tom, Rocky and Henryin 2003. This company represents Michel second adventure in the chocolate industry. Michel has been roasting and blending specialty chocolates in Midwest countries since 1982 and is highly regarded within the industry as an expert in the field. We source the finest cocoa from the most highly regarded growing regions and farms from around the world. These cacoa's are custom roasted to order, packaged same day and shipped within 24 hours. Our company has grown into one of the largest specialty Chocolate Producers in the Midwest on the back bone of our reputation for the quality, freshness, and unmatched customer service. Our Cleveland roots have given us a blue collar work ethic and appreciation for creating consistently outstanding chocolates our customers have come to appreciate and expect.
